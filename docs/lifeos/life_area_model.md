# Declutr Life Area Model Guide

Life Areas represent high-level domains of human activity.

## 12 Default Life Areas

- **Personal**: Personal identity and journal context
- **Work**: Employment and professional responsibilities
- **Business**: Entrepreneurship, startups, and companies
- **Education**: Degrees, courses, and academia
- **Finance**: Banking, taxes, and investments
- **Health**: Medical records, insurance, and fitness
- **Travel**: Trips, itineraries, and passports
- **Legal**: Contracts, agreements, and IDs
- **Home**: Real estate, maintenance, and utility bills
- **Family**: Relatives, shared household documents
- **Research**: Academic papers and investigations
- **Hobbies**: Creative projects and leisure activities
